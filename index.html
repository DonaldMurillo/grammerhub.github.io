GRAMMERHUB

is a meetup for emerging tech talent: junior and mid-level software developers, designers, product and project managers plus those exploring a switch to Tech. Welcome!

STREAMING LIVE MONDAYS @ 6:30 PM EST

Our goals for Grammerhub's Weekly Monday sessions are:

  • Support independent projects and collaboration
  • Build a community and provide accountability
  • Provide opportunities to grow professionally in Tech

New to Grammerhub? No problem! We have volunteer organizers to help you get started.

The Grammerhub Community is on Discord: discord.grammerhub.org
